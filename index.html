Lake Tahoe, California

LAKE TAHOE is one of the most breathtaking attractions located in California. It is home to a number of ski resorts, Summer outdoor recreation, and tourist attractions. Snow and skiing are a significant part of the area's reputation.

Pack Accordingly

One of the most important things when it comes to travelling through the great outdoors is packing accordingly. Here are a few tips:

  1. Bring layers of clothing
  2. Pack sunscreen
  3. Carry extra water just in case
  4. Pack light
